About

I'm Vishnu, an IC design engineer passionate about bridging the gap between abstract algorithms and physical silicon. My work spans the full design stack — from high-level RTL to transistor-level analog circuits.

With deep expertise in CMOS circuit design, I specialize in building power-efficient architectures for high-performance applications. I thrive at the intersection of digital and analog — where the real challenges live.

Currently focused on low-power mixed-signal design, data converters, and PLL architectures for next-generation SoCs. Always chasing that elusive balance between speed, power, and area.
